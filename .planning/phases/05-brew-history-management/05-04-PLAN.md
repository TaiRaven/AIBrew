**Pre-condition:** Ensure at least 2 brew_log records exist in the instance. If fewer than 2

--- RPT-01: History List ---

1. Log in as aibrew_user (non-admin)
2. Open the app from the ServiceNow navigator
3. Tap the "History" tab in TopNav — verify it is no longer greyed out and navigation succeeds
4. Verify the page heading shows "Brew History" and a count chip (e.g. "3 brews")
5. Verify brew cards appear in reverse-chronological order (newest at top)
6. Verify each card shows: date·time (e.g. "May 7 · 08:14"), method chip, bean name,
   dose·water·ratio line (e.g. "18g • 40g • 1:2.2"), and rating if rated
7. Verify that a brew with no rating shows NO rating segment (not "★ 0/10")
8. If fewer than 50 brews exist: "Load more" button should NOT appear
9. If 50+ brews exist: verify "Load more" appears; tap it and verify new cards APPEND
   below existing ones (earlier cards must still be visible)
10. Navigate to HomeView — verify "History" tile is active and tapping it routes to ?view=history

--- BREW-10: Edit ---

11. Tap any brew card to open the edit modal
12. Verify ALL fields are pre-populated from the record:
    - Method chip matching the brew's method is highlighted
    - Bean picker shows the brew's bean selected
    - Dose and Water inputs show correct gram values, live ratio is displayed
    - Grind size input shows correct value
    - Equipment picker shows the brew's equipment
    - Brew time shows in mm:ss format (e.g. "1:28" for 88 seconds)
    - Rating circles show the brew's rating pre-selected
    - Taste notes textarea shows the brew's notes
13. Change one field (e.g. tap a different rating circle)
14. Tap "Save changes" — modal must close, list must re-fetch showing the updated value on the card
15. Tap Cancel — modal closes, NO change to record (verify original value still present)
16. Tap a card, close and re-open without saving — verify modal shows ORIGINAL values (not stale edited values)

--- BREW-11: Delete ---

17. Tap the trash icon (🗑) on any brew card — a confirmation modal must appear
    with "Delete this brew?" and "This cannot be undone." text
18. Tap Cancel — modal closes, record still present in list
19. Tap the trash icon again — confirm modal — tap Delete
    - Record must disappear from the list
    - List must re-fetch (count chip decrements)
20. Navigate to the bean detail page for the deleted brew's bean
    - Verify the stock figure (remaining grams) increased by the dose_weight_g of the deleted brew
21. Open any brew card in the edit modal
22. Tap "Delete brew" text button at top of edit modal — confirmation modal appears
23. Tap Cancel — confirmation modal closes, edit modal still open (edit modal must NOT close)
24. Tap "Delete brew" again — confirm — tap Delete — both modals close, record gone from list

--- ACL Gate (non-admin required) ---

25. With aibrew_user (non-admin): confirm the above steps all worked (read + write + delete access)
26. Log out of aibrew_user. Log in as a user WITHOUT the aibrew_user role (if available)
    — attempt to navigate to the app — verify access is denied or records are not visible
  </how-to-verify>
